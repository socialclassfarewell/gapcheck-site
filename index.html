Gapcheck

Audit-readiness gap checks for NDIS providers

What it is

Gapcheck reviews your organisation-level documentation — policies, procedures, registers and training records — against the NDIS Practice Standards modules that apply to your registration groups. You receive a clear, prioritised gap report for auditor preparation, at a fixed price agreed before work begins.

Scope

Organisation-level documentation only. Participant files, clinical records and individual worker performance are not reviewed. Gapcheck is preparation support — it is not an audit, certification, or legal advice.

How your documents are handled

Documents stay in Australia. No external AI upload of your policies.

Who runs it

Gapcheck was founded by a registered nurse with clinical governance experience, based in Queensland.

Contact
